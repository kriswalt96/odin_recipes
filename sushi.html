Sushi

sushi, a staple rice dish of Japanese cuisine, consisting of cooked rice flavoured with vinegar and a variety of vegetable, egg, or raw seafood garnishes and served cold.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fish
  • Wasabi Paste
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ginger

Steps to make Sushi

  1. Cook rice in water until it boils
  2. Meanwhile, chop filling into strips or sticks
  3. Mix sugar and vinegar
  4. On your sushi bamboo mat, spread out 1 sheet of nori
  5. Cover it as thinly as you can with the rice mixture
